History

Ninjata Rafting Company was founded in 2019 with a passion for adventure and a deep respect for nature. What began as a small group of enthusiasts has grown into a trusted rafting operator known for delivering safe and thrilling river experiences for all skill levels.

With certified and committed guides, quality equipment, and a commitment to eco-friendly practices, we create unforgettable journeys that connect people to the power and beauty of the river. Today, we continue to inspire adventure, foster camaraderie, and promote respect for the wild waters we ride.
